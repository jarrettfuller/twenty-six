Who we are

twenty-six is run by Jarrett Fuller and a rotating cast of collaborators. Jarrett is a designer, writer, and educator. He hosts the weekly podcast, Scratching the Surface, and teaches in Pratt Institute's graduate program and Parsons School of Design's undergraduate program. He has previously worked as a designer at Facebook, Warby Parker, and The Whitney Museum of American Art. He received in MFA in graphic design and critical studies from the Maryland Institute College of Art.
