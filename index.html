Principal

Jarrett Fuller is the founder and principal of twenty-six, editorial director for Design Observer, host of Scratching the Service, and adjunct faculty at TK. He has previously worked as a designer at Facebook, Warby Parker, and The Whitney Museum of American Art. He received in MFA in graphic design and critical studies from the Maryland Institute College of Art.
